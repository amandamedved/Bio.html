Amanda Medved

Recent College Graduate Looking to Start a Career in Writing



Hi, my name is Amanda Medved and I am a recent graduate of the University of Washington in Seattle. I graduated with a Bachelor's degree in English and a minor in Environmental Studies. I am passionate about all things writing, and am especially interested in Technical Writing and UX Writing.

I was born and raised in Seattle, Washington, and currently have plans to stay here for at least the next year as I work out my transition into the workforce. I grew up in a small neighborhood called Magnolia, and attended Seattle Preparatory High School before UW. Writing and analyzing text has always come easy to me, and I have always known that I wanted to be a writer in whatever career I choose for myself. When I was in high school, I had a teacher who taught me about the climate crisis and the importance of living sustainably. Ever since then, I have been passionate about promoting more sustainable lifestyles, and have a large interest in corporate sustainability.

In my free time, I love to binge new TV shows and movies, take yoga classes, spend time with family and friends, and travel. When the COVID-19 pandemic hit last year, I was studying abroad in Europe. Luckily, I was able to spend two and a half months there traveling to ten different countries before we were called home by the travel bans. Traveling abroad was one of my most cherished and enriching life experiences, and I will never forget fleeing from Prague in the middle of the night to get back home to the U.S.

I am currently taking a few classes to improve my skills in Technical Writing and UX Writing. My goals for the future are to find a full-time role or internship in one of these fields, and continue improving upon my skills and learning new things. If you would like to connect, please feel free to reach out via email, or check out my portfolio website.
